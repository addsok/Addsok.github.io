About Us

Grim’s Trims is a local grounds maintenance business based in South Yorkshire, working across Sheffield, Rotherham and the surrounding areas. We’re here to help keep your garden or outdoor space tidy all year round – whether it’s grass cutting, hedge trimming, garden clearance, tree work, or green waste removal.

We offer one-off jobs or regular fortnightly/monthly visits, depending on what you need. No nonsense, no messing about – just reliable, honest work from a local team that takes pride in what we do.

If your garden needs sorting, give Grim’s Trims a shout – we’ll get it looking right in no time.
